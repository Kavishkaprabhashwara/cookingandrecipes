01.Coconut Sambol (Pol Sambol)

Ingredients:

Grated coconut – 100g,Dried red chili – 8 to 12,Small onion / Shallots – 5 to 7,Lime – ½,Sugar – ¼ teaspoon,Salt – As you need

Cooking instructions:

1.Get ready with grated coconut, dried red chilies, onion, salt, sugar, and lime

2.Grind dried red chilies and salt together to flakes as shown in the picture below.

3.Wash the mortar and pestle. Then, add the grated coconut and ground dried red chili flakes. Bash and muddle them using a pestle until they combined well.

4.Now add sugar and bash them again

5.inally, add the onions and bash them to a good mix.

6.Taste the sambal and adjust salt if needed.

7. Now, take off all the mixture to a plate or bowl. Then, add the lime juice and mix everything well.

This is how to make an easy coconut sambal recipe at home. Serve and enjoy this Sri Lankan style pol sambol with dried red chili.


02.Chili paste (Katta Sambol/Lunu Miris)

Ingredients:

½ tsp Olive oil optional,1 medium -size Onion,1 tbsp Chili flakes,10-15 or even 20 Dried red chili pepper,1 Green chili,½ Lime juice,⅓ tsp Salt or as needed,½ tbsp Maldives fish or as needed

Cooking instructions:

1.Grind all of the above ingredients together using a blender. I used the magic bullet to blend them until everything is crushed into bits and pieces.

2.You may grind it all the way to a paste if you prefer or grind it well enough to feel the bits and pieces. This way you will be able to taste the crunch of the maldive fish.

3.When it is done blending all the ingredients well, serve as a side with your favourite meal to add extra flavours.


03.Brinjal Pickle (Wambatu Moju))

Ingredients:

8 Brinjal I used small purple ones,¼ cup Shallots Small onion,8 cloves Garlic,2 Green chilli,1 teaspoon Sugar,Salt,1 sprig Curry leaves,Oil For deep frying + tossing

Mix together:

1 teaspoon Ginger garlic paste,½ teaspoon Red chilli powder,½ teaspoon Mustard powder,½ teaspoon Vinegar

Cooking instructions:

1.Wash and slice firstly. Marinate by coating with turmeric and salt.

2.Set aside for 15 minutes.

3.After that, gently mix by squeezing without too much pressure. Squeeze the excess water.

4.Set the squeezed brinjals aside.

Deep frying:

1.Heat enough oil for deep frying. Using a strainer ladle, give shallots a dip.

2.ust few seconds, drain from oil over kitchen towel.

3.Dip garlic and green chilli quickly in the oil next. Drain over paper towel.

4.Lastly fry brinjals in batches until golden.

5.Drain over paper towel and set aside.

Mix together:

*Place all the ingredients under 'Mix together' to get a spice paste. 1 teaspoon Ginger garlic paste,½ teaspoon Red chilli powder,½ teaspoon Vinegar,½ teaspoon Mustard powder

Tossing:

1.Heat a pan with 1 tablespoon of oil. Add curry leaves to it and fry until crisp.

2.Now, add the paste prepared with couple of tablespoons of water.

3.dd sugar and a little bit of salt. Cook until oil oozes.

4.In goes the fried items.

5.Mix together to coat well.

Once evenly mixed, switch off the flame.
